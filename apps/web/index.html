Why OmniDub

      
    
        
  • Emotion preserved. Angry stays angry. Whisper stays whisper. Every line is tagged from the speaker's face and delivery, then piped into MiMo's (tag) control syntax.
    • 
        
  • Voice identity preserved. Each speaker is cloned from their own clip — 3 speakers in the video means 3 distinct voices in the dub, not one narrator.
    • 
        
  • Timing preserved. The translator iterates until the target-language line fits the original shot ±8%, so the dub never runs over the next cut.
    • 
        
  • Zero extra models. No Whisper, no ElevenLabs, no pyannote. Just MiMo-V2.5 and ffmpeg.
